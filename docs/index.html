How does seasonality work ?

Each season lasts for 1 month.

During the month, each fish can be either 'in season' or 'out of season' (the timing varies for each species).

When a fish is Out Of Season, its maximum weight does not exceed 20% of the species' maximum weight. In other words, this fish is limited to a maximum of 1 star.

When a fish is in season, its weight is not limited, and the fish can reach up to 5 stars.

Points

How does points and weights work ?

Each fish has a minimum and maximum weight range.

When a player catches a fish, its weight will fall within these limits, determining the number of points earned and the fish's star rating, ranging from 1 to 5 stars. Considering that the minimum weight corresponds to 0% and the maximum weight to 100%, we divide this range into 5 equal parts of 20% each.

a one star fish will have a weight between 0% and 20%.

a two stars fish will have a weight between 20% and 40%.

etc.

The point ranges are as follows:

Common
min 300 -> max 600
Rare
min 400 -> max 800
Epic
min 500 -> max 1000
Monster
min 5000 -> max 10000
Therefore, an epic fish with a weight of 30% will be worth 700 points and have a quality rating of 2 stars.
